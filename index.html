Why Durban?

If you want a warm home away from home. Durban is the place for you!

Durban is known as the busiest port in Africa and a major hub for container trade, is the seat of the larger eThekwini Metropolitan Municipality, which spans a large area and has a large population, has a rich, diverse heritage, with large Zulu, Indian, White, and Coloured populations, and it's a major national and international tourism destination because of its warm subtropical climate and extensive beaches. Durban is also the headquarters of South Africa's sugar industry. It is located on the east coast of South Africa in the KwaZulu-Natal province. It is a sophisticated cosmopolitan city, known for its beaches and warm weather all year round, is home to a large Indian community, evident in the architecture and spice market of the city, and is often referred to as "Little India". Durban is known for its relaxed lifestyle, making it a great place to slow down and enjoy the coastal environment. It has a world-renowned stadium, known as Moses Mabhida stadium which is a major landmark in Durban and hosts various events (See picture displayed).
